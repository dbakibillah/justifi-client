Arbitration Agreement

By initiating an Arbitration process on the JustiFi platform, you ("the User" or "the Party") agree to be legally bound by the following terms, rules, and procedures.

Important Notice

These terms constitute a legally binding agreement. Please read them carefully before proceeding with arbitration services.

Article 1: Arbitration Request and Fees

Request Fee

A non-refundable Arbitration Request Fee of BDT 5,000 must be paid at the time of submitting the arbitration request.

Non-Refundable Policy

This fee is for administrative costs and is non-refundable under any circumstances, including if arbitration does not proceed or if a settlement is reached early.

Arbitration Process

1

Payment & Submission

Pay the BDT 5,000 request fee while submitting your arbitration request through our platform.

2

Agreement Session

We will contact all parties to schedule an online meeting where we'll discuss and confirm:

  • Total arbitration cost (negotiable)
  • Number of prospective sessions
  • Selection of arbitrators from our panel
  • Session duration and timelines
  • Award compliance period

Parties will provide digital signatures during this session.

3

Formal Agreement

After the session, we'll create a formal arbitration agreement and send it to all parties via email.

4

Arbitration Sessions

Sessions will proceed according to the agreed procedure and timeline.

5

Final Verdict & Award

After the final session, the arbitrator will issue a binding award that is enforceable by law.

Article 3: Process Termination

Mutual Termination Option

If after some sessions, parties mutually decide they will not continue the process, they can terminate further sessions by informing JustiFi.

Cost Responsibility

Parties will pay only for the sessions they have completed up to the termination point.

Termination Processing Cost

A lump-sum amount will be added to cover administrative costs associated with process termination.

Article 5: Issue Resolution

Our Commitment to You

JustiFi always thinks the best for you so that your legal journey is better with us. If you face any issue during the process, we're here to help.

3-Day Issue Resolution Window

If you inform us about any issues within 3 days of occurrence, JustiFi will judge the issue and take appropriate action to resolve your concern.

Prompt Communication

We encourage you to communicate any concerns immediately so we can address them promptly.

Fair Assessment

JustiFi will fairly assess all reported issues and take appropriate measures to ensure a smooth arbitration process.

Article 4: Cost Distribution

Equal
Distribution

Equal Sharing Principle

The total cost of arbitration, as finalized during the Agreement Session, shall be distributed equally among all parties involved.

Unless otherwise unanimously agreed upon in writing by all parties and recorded in the Arbitration Agreement.

Article 6: User Conduct and Prohibited Activities

Professional Demeanor

All parties must act respectfully and professionally towards the Arbitrator, JustiFi staff, and other parties throughout the process.

Prohibited Activities

  • Breaching the confidentiality of the proceedings
  • Recording sessions without express permission
  • Engaging in harassment, intimidation, or deceit

Consequences of Violations

Any breach of these conduct rules may lead to immediate termination of the process by JustiFi and may result in legal action for damages.
